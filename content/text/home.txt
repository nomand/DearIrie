Irie is a 33' cutter rigged, full keel custom built Tahitiana.
She is 12 tonnes, made of steel, with a beautifully crafted teak interior. 
Irie is a heavy displacement expedition style sailboat designed for high latitude sailing.
The legend has it that it was built for a trip to Antarctica.

```
Irie is currently for sale and looking for a new owner.
Located in a liveaboard boatyard in Whangarei, New Zealand.
```
