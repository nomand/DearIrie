Cockpit

New cockpit drain steel and two patches needs welding in. Old seats were screwed in from below. Those holes need filling in to make cockpit water-tight and seats re-built.

Port

Most of the problem rust areas are around companionway. Wooden hardwaware like hatch cover and rails were bolted through, requiring a massive ceiling disassembly to get to. I propose cutting out and welding a hole sheet in and mounting the cover and the wooden slider rails on stainless tabs instead, making it easy to take off for service.

Foredeck

Engine

Irie needs a new engine, driveshaft and associated throttle controls fuel and exhaust plumbing.

Engine access is phenomenal for a boat her size that makes it easy to look after. She previously had a 1998 Yanmar 3HMF 28hp inboard diesel.

A brand new Bete30 is available for sale separately.

Lasdrop Gen 1 1/4" Shaft | 2 1/4" Stern Tube dripless shaft seal is on board and ready to install.

Electrical

Irie is equipped with a 440ah lead acid battery bank made up of 2S2P 6V 220 cells.

Irie needs a partial rewire to create a tidy layout, managed by a classic BlueSea systems electical switchboard.

Refrigiration unit needs to be diagnosed for leaks. The compressor unit is operational, with a spare unit on board.
